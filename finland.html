About Studying in Finland

Education System

  • World-renowned education system
  • Innovative teaching methods
  • Research-focused programs
  • English-taught degrees available
  • Practical, hands-on learning

Student Life

  • Safe and peaceful environment
  • Stunning natural landscapes
  • Northern Lights experiences
  • Rich cultural heritage
  • Student-friendly cities

Practical Information

  • Residence permit required for non-EU students
  • Tuition fees for non-EU students: €4,000 - €18,000/year
  • Living costs: €700 - €1,000/month
  • Part-time work allowed (25 hours/week)
  • Health insurance required
