About Studying in Italy

Education System

  • World's oldest university system
  • Specialized in arts, design, and architecture
  • Strong focus on research
  • English-taught programs available
  • Rich academic heritage

Student Life

  • Rich cultural experiences
  • World-renowned cuisine
  • Historic cities and architecture
  • Vibrant social scene
  • Travel opportunities throughout Europe

Practical Information

  • Student visa required for non-EU students
  • Tuition fees: €900 - €4,000/year
  • Living costs: €700 - €1,000/month
  • Part-time work allowed (20 hours/week)
  • Health insurance required
