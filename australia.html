About Studying in Australia

Education System

  • World-class universities
  • Innovative teaching methods
  • Research opportunities
  • Practical, career-focused education
  • Globally recognized qualifications

Student Life

  • Diverse multicultural society
  • Beautiful landscapes and beaches
  • Outdoor lifestyle
  • Work opportunities during studies
  • High quality of life

Practical Information

  • Student Visa (subclass 500) required
  • Tuition: AUD 20,000 - AUD 45,000/year
  • Living costs: AUD 21,041/year
  • Post-study work rights available
  • Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC) required
