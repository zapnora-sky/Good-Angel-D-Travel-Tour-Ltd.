US Student Visa (F-1) Requirements

Complete guide to obtaining your F-1 visa for studying in the United States

F-1 Visa Requirements

Required Documents

  • Valid passport
  • Form I-20 from your school
  • DS-160 confirmation page
  • Visa application fee receipt
  • SEVIS fee receipt
  • Passport-style photograph
  • Academic transcripts
  • Standardized test scores
  • Financial evidence

Financial Requirements

  • Proof of sufficient funds for first year
  • Evidence of funding for subsequent years
  • Bank statements (last 3-6 months)
  • Sponsorship letters if applicable
  • Scholarship award letters
  • Estimated costs: $25,000 - $70,000/year

Visa Interview

  • Prepare to explain your study plans
  • Demonstrate strong ties to home country
  • Explain how you will fund your education
  • Discuss post-study plans
  • Answer questions clearly and honestly
  • Bring all original documents

Application Timeline

1

3-6 Months Before

Receive I-20 from school, pay SEVIS fee

2

2-3 Months Before

Complete DS-160, pay visa fee, schedule interview

3

1-2 Months Before

Gather documents, prepare for interview

4

Interview

Attend visa interview at US Embassy/Consulate

5

After Approval

Receive passport with visa, make travel arrangements
