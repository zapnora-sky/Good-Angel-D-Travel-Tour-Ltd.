About Studying in Portugal

Education System

  • High-quality education system
  • Affordable tuition fees
  • English-taught programs available
  • Strong focus on research
  • Modern facilities and resources

Student Life

  • Beautiful beaches and coastline
  • Rich history and culture
  • Mild climate year-round
  • Friendly and welcoming locals
  • Affordable cost of living

Practical Information

  • Student visa required for non-EU students
  • Tuition fees: €1,000 - €7,000/year
  • Living costs: €600 - €900/month
  • Part-time work allowed (20 hours/week)
  • Health insurance required
