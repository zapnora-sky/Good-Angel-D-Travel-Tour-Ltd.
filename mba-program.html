MBA in International Business

Harvard Business School - Transform your career with a world-class MBA education

Program Overview

The MBA in International Business at Harvard Business School is designed for aspiring global leaders who want to make a significant impact in the world of business. This prestigious program combines rigorous academic training with real-world application, preparing students for leadership roles in multinational corporations, international organizations, and global startups.

Program Highlights

  • World-renowned faculty with extensive industry experience
  • Global immersion experiences in key business markets
  • Case-based learning methodology
  • Extensive alumni network of business leaders
  • Career services with global recruitment opportunities
  • Entrepreneurship and innovation focus

Learning Outcomes

Upon completion of this program, graduates will be able to:

  • Develop and implement global business strategies
  • Lead cross-cultural teams effectively
  • Analyze and navigate complex international markets
  • Make data-driven decisions in uncertain environments
  • Create sustainable business models for global impact

Program Curriculum

Year 1: Core Foundation

First Semester

  • Financial Reporting and Control
  • Leadership and Organizational Behavior
  • Marketing Management
  • Technology and Operations Management
  • Business, Government and International Economy

Second Semester

  • Strategy
  • Leadership and Corporate Accountability
  • Finance
  • The Entrepreneurial Manager
  • Field Global Immersion
Year 2: Specialization & Electives

International Business Focus

  • Global Strategic Management
  • Cross-Cultural Leadership
  • International Marketing Strategy
  • Global Supply Chain Management
  • Emerging Markets Strategy

Elective Courses

  • Digital Transformation
  • Social Enterprise
  • Negotiation and Conflict Resolution
  • Business Analytics
  • Venture Capital and Private Equity
Experiential Learning

Global Immersion Programs

  • Asia-Pacific Business immersion (2 weeks)
  • European Market Study Tour (2 weeks)
  • Latin America Emerging Markets Project

Capstone Project

Students work on real-world international business challenges with partner organizations, developing comprehensive business solutions.

Admission Requirements

Academic Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree from accredited institution
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0/4.0 or equivalent
  • Official academic transcripts
  • GMAT score of 650+ or GRE equivalent

Application Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Statement of purpose
  • Professional resume/CV
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Proof of English proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL)

Professional Experience

  • Minimum 2 years of work experience
  • Professional achievements
  • Leadership experience
  • International exposure (preferred)
